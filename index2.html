ABOUT

We help tree owners grow healthy fruit, provide assistance in harvesting and preserving fruit, promote the sharing of extra fruit, and work to protect urban fruit trees.

City Fruit is a non-profit corporation with a tax exempt status (501c[3]), supported by donations, memberships, class fees, sales and grants. Show your support by joining today.
